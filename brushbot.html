LED Wearable (version 1)



Artist Statement

My idea for this project is to put led lights to my bike helmet to hopefully add more safety to my bike ride, especially when I'm riding in the dark.
I placed it on my right handle. For additional safety, when I press on the brakes, the helmet lights will be activated.
This project was more technical compared to the brushbot. I faced different challenges such as connectivity issues.
After I presented my working project in class, a couple of lights went out and I had to figure out what was wrong with it.
I ended up changing and resoldering the parts because I wasn't sure if the led died, or if the wires that weren't supposed to touch were touching.
I couldn't figure out what was wrong with it. I just redid everything and somehow it worked.
