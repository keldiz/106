Artist Statement
My goal for this project was to make a device for my workout equipments.
I want it to talk to me (through the led display) as I do reps. I also
want the lights to animate as I lift the weights. Doing this project was very
challenging. We learned how to use arduino but I had difficulty combining
the codes together at first. With the help of Steve, I was able to achieve my goals and more.
The best part about this project is that it is something that I can use even after this class.
A lot of times, art projects are not used or kept in the storage after the semester.
I never imagined building an eletronic that I can use everyday.
